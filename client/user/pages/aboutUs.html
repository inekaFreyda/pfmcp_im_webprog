PFMCP: NATURE AND GOALS


The Philippine Federation of Married Catholic Priests, Inc.,(PFMCP) is a non-government, non-stock, and non-profit association committed to the tasks of working for the renewal of the church particularly of priestly ministry and to be involved in the empowerment of peoples and enhancement of the environment.

To do these, PFMCP, Inc., among others shall:

  1. Unite the local groups of married catholic perists, their wives and children in the Philippines.
  2. Link with other married priests' organizations of other countries and the international federation of married catholic priests;
  3. Encourage local groups to organize their own activities and programs with their own vision, mission, and goals. Local organizations may register their own organizations with the Securities and Exchange Comission;
  4. Strengthen the national organization with the common goals, speaking in one voice in major issues and setting up of projects and activities related to its purpose and goals.

More particularly, PFMCP, Inc., would pursue the following goals:

  1. Participate in the renewal an transformation of the church;
  2. Work for the plurality of priestly forms;
  3. Promote the dignity of marriage and family life;
  4. Involve in the process of sustained development of Philippine society;
  5. Actually work for acceptance of married priest into full communion with the catholic church;
  6. Hold activities that is family and people-oriented;
  7. Enlighten the public mind concerning the movement of married priests through the use of media.
  8. The Federation is placed under the patronage of the Holy Family. It is affiliated with the Internation Federation of Married Catholic Priests and links other national groups of other countries.


PFMCP, Inc., seeks to implement its purpose and goals through correspondence among member organizations and other individuals and groups, hold seminars and trainings, do publications, give consultancy and hold conferences and meetings.
